Ecstasy with Soft, Yummy Dairy-Free & Vegan Ice Cream

Dairy-free and vegan ice creams are frozen desserts made without any animal-based dairy ingredients. Instead of milk and cream, these ice creams use plant-based alternatives like almond milk, oat milk, coconut milk, cashew milk, and soy milk.
